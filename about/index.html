Who We Are

Our Mission

Our mission is to help organizations turn data into insight and insight into action. Through rigorous research, thoughtful evaluation, and strategic consulting, we support partners in navigating complexity and making informed decisions.

We combine advanced analytical methods with tools like surveys, focus groups, and in-depth interviews to uncover key opportunities and challenges. Our work promotes clarity, supports inclusive engagement, and helps drive meaningful progress across sectors—empowering clients to lead with confidence and purpose.

Our Vision

We envision a world where data-driven insight leads to smarter decisions, stronger organizations, and meaningful progress. Through research, analysis, and collaboration, we help organizations identify opportunities, address challenges, and create environments where all people can thrive.

Sensemaking Lab is committed to being a trusted partner for positive change, equipping mission-driven teams with the knowledge, tools, and strategies they need to lead with clarity, confidence, and purpose.

Our Approach

Sensemaking Lab takes a thoughtful, evidence-based approach to research, evaluation, and strategy. We apply both established and innovative methods to ensure our work is accurate, relevant, and reflective of the diverse contexts in which our clients operate.

Our team uses advanced statistical techniques, computational tools, and mixed-methods designs—including surveys, interviews, and focus groups—to generate a deep understanding of complex challenges. This approach helps organizations make informed decisions and develop solutions that are grounded, effective, and inclusive.
